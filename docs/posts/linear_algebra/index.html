Area of Triangle

$$S_{\mathrm{triangle}}=\frac{1}{2} \left| \begin{vmatrix} x_0 & x_1 & x_2\\ y_0 & y_1 & y_2\\ 1 & 1 & 1 \end{vmatrix}\right|$$

Area of triangle in complex number form

$$S_{\mathrm{triangle}}=\frac{i}{4} \left| \begin{vmatrix} 1 & 1 & 1\\ z_1 & z_2 & z_3\\ \bar{z_1} & \bar{z_2} & \bar{z_3} \end{vmatrix}\right|$$

Volume of Tetrahedron

$$V_{\mathrm{tetrahedron}}=\frac{1}{6} \left| \begin{vmatrix} x_0 & x_1 & x_2 & x_3\\ y_0 & y_1 & y_2 & y_3\\ z_0 & z_1 & z_2 & z_3\\ 1 & 1 & 1 & 1 \end{vmatrix}\right|$$

Two Vector Cross Product

Gereral Quadratic Curve

3 Points Determine a Circle
