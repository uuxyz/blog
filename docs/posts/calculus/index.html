volumes of revolution

curvature

$$k=\frac{|f^{\prime\prime}|}{\sqrt{[1+(f^\prime)^{2}]^{3}}}$$ $$\left(\frac{x-x_0}{1+(f^\prime)^2}+\frac{f^\prime}{f^{\prime\prime}}\right)^2+\left(\frac{y-y_0}{1+(f^\prime)^2}-\frac{1}{f^{\prime\prime}}\right)^2=\frac{1+(f^\prime)^2}{(f^{\prime\prime})^2}$$
